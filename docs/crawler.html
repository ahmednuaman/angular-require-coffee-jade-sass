    This is a simple recursive crawler that users PhatomJS to create snap shots of your site and save them as static, SEO friendly HTML files.

    The crawler works on the basis that your app’s URLs are RESTful and compensates for this design by saving the generated document’s HTML in an index.html file. This means that if your URL is /foo/bar, it can then be accessed via /foo/bar/ and /foo/bar/index.html in addition to the original schema,
